Coming from systems work changes how I look at web apps.

I still think in terms of constraints, efficieny, observability, and predictable behavior. In full-stack projects, that translates to clear structure, low cognitive load, and resilient defaults.

This blog section is a step toward sharing those ideas with real project notes and postmortems.
